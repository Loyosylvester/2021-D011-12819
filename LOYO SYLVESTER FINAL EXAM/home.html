  • YOUR PARTNER IN UGANDA'S OIL AND GAS SECTOR

  • Uganda National Oil Company - UNOC was incorporated as a limited liability company wholly owned by the Government of Uganda in June 2015. Our overall function is to handle the Government of Uganda’s commercial interests in the petroleum sub-sector and to ensure State participation in petroleum activities. The company is also mandated to investigate and propose new upstream, midstream, and downstream ventures initially locally and eventually internationally. UNOC now invites applications from qualified Ugandans who fully meet the required job specifications and with the right personal attributes and a passion for excellence to occupy the following positions in the Company’s established structure

    All the positions indicated attract a competitive remuneration package for the successful candidates.
